Keyboard/Mouse Instructions:

Press 'IJKL' to move the camera's direction horizontally/vertically.
Press ←↑→↓ to move the camera sideways/up/down.
Press '+/-' to move the camera forward/backward.
Drag your mouse to change the world lamp position.
Press 'C/X' to toggle the two lamps.
Press 'M' to change materials.
Press '2/3' to switch between Gouroud and Phong shading.
Press 'B' to switch between Blinn-Phong & Phong lighting.
